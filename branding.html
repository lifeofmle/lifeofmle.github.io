"ICE" Rebranding

I was tasked to come up with a logo design for a system that had the acronym of I.C.E. The existing splash screen was a picture of a glacier and the logo was just the words ICE. To bring the modernized the logo, I asked the stakeholders of different ideas they had for application logo and splash screen.
