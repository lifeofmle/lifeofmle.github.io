Hello

My name is Michael Le

Currently leading UX and design at Kounta

Previously designing at Pivotal Labs, The Telegraph (UK), and Tobias and Tobias.

Code meets design

I graduated from the University of Waterloo in Computer Engineering and have worked in New York City, London, and now am based in Sydney, Australia.

My coding background helps me understand the technical aspects of a project and allows for more options when prototyping solutions.

I am a self taught designer with a curious mind and desire to find out the "why" that links people and products. Over the years I have acquired the skills to lead teams with conducting product discovery research, usability testing, quantitative testing (A/B), and visual design from wireframes to polished design assets.
